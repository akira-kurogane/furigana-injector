Set the furigana preferences to ignore the most common kanji.


Explanation

Words that are only composed of easy kanji will not be processed for furigana. If you want to see the exact list that have been defined as easy for you, open the options panel and look at the 'Exclusion Kanji' list.

To adjust the settings for individual kanji whilst you read web pages, select the word with your text, and then right-click to see the context menu.
